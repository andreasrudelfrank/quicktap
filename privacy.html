Privacy Policy — Quick Tap

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Quick Tap does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. Quick Tap has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no trackers. We do not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

Network access

The app makes no network connections of any kind. It works fully offline — you can play it in Airplane Mode.

On-device data

Your best score (high score) is saved locally on your device using the system's standard UserDefaults storage. This value never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Third parties

Quick Tap contains no third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics services.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
